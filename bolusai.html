BolusAI — the smart rumen capsule

Fit once, monitor for years. BolusAI sits safely in the cow's rumen and gives you early visibility of heat and health — from the inside, where it matters.

How it works

BolusAI is administered orally like a standard bolus and settles in the rumen. From there it continuously measures temperature, activity and rumen conditions, transmitting data wirelessly to a base station and on to the cloud.

  • 1. Administer — a one-time, stress-free application per animal
  • 2. Monitor — the capsule records data around the clock
  • 3. Analyse — our AI detects heat events and health anomalies
  • 4. Alert — you get a clear notification on your phone

How the system works — end to end

Enterprise-grade architecture, built for farms: ultra-low-power LoRaWAN from the capsule to mesh-linked farm gateways, encrypted internet uplink to the TechAIML cloud, and AI-driven dashboards and alerts on any device.

Why LoRaWAN?

We chose LoRaWAN because it's the proven industry standard for large-scale, low-power IoT — ideal for working farms.

  • ~500 m coverage per gateway — aerial radius from a centrally mounted gateway, covering barns, yards and paddocks
  • Mesh networking — add gateways and they link together, extending coverage across larger farms and remote fields
  • Ultra-low power — the capsule transmits for years without a battery change
  • Scales with your herd — each gateway handles thousands of capsules
  • Secure by design — end-to-end encryption from capsule to cloud
  • Resilient — gateways buffer data locally if the internet connection drops
