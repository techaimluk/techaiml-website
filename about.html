About TechAIML

A UK technology company on a mission to make precision agriculture practical for every farm.

Our story

TechAIML Ltd was founded on a simple observation: farming generates enormous amounts of valuable information every day, but most of it goes unmeasured. We build Internet of Things and AI technology that captures that information and turns it into decisions farmers can act on.

Our flagship product, BolusAI, monitors cattle from inside the rumen — giving farmers early visibility of heat cycles and health issues that would otherwise go unnoticed until it's too late or too expensive.

What we believe

Technology should work for the farmer, not the other way round. Our solutions are designed to be fitted once and trusted — no fiddly maintenance, no steep learning curves, no unnecessary hardware.

Prevention beats cure. Early detection of heat and health events saves money, improves animal welfare and reduces reliance on emergency interventions.

Data means nothing without interpretation. We invest heavily in AI and analytics so that what reaches your phone is a clear, actionable alert — not a spreadsheet of raw numbers.

Our experience

Agriculture is our focus, but not where our story starts. Our team has delivered technology services across some of the most demanding industries — banking and financial services, telecoms, and automotive software development among them. That background means we bring enterprise-grade engineering discipline, security and reliability to every solution we build for the farm.

Where we work

We're based in the United Kingdom and work with dairy and beef farmers, vets and agricultural partners across the UK and beyond, alongside clients in other industries who need dependable IoT and software solutions.
