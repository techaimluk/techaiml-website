Meet BolusAI — see what's happening inside

BolusAI is a smart rumen capsule that sits safely in the cow's rumen and continuously measures what external sensors can't.

  • Early, accurate heat (oestrus) detection for better fertility results
  • Continuous temperature and activity monitoring, 24/7
  • Health alerts straight to your phone, before visible symptoms
  • Fit once — no collars, tags or batteries to swap
